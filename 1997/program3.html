Acknowledgments

Tet Trung Thu is presented by the Mid-Autumn Festival Committee, a Committee overseen by the parent organization, Viet-American Forum. The Committee is headed by three Board of Directors who are responsible for the festival planning. The Committee is divided into six subcommittees consisting of: coordination, administration, marketing, programming, finance, and construction. College and high school students of Northern California make up about 200 volunteers. The students are determined to use their leadership and organizational skills to put together an event that will benefit the development of their community.

There are several motivating factors behind the annual holding of the Festival. The members of the Mid- Autumn Festival Committee are dedicated to cultural and ethnic awareness. This is an event that gives families, friends, and the community exchanges, where various ethnic groups within the community (Vietnamese, Chinese, Hispanic, etc.) come together to share their culture through artwork, performance, and music. Games, workshops, and a number of other activities help to promote peaceful relationships among all the ethnic groups.
